Where does the template get inserted?

When a state is activated, its templates are automatically inserted into the ui-view of its parent state's template. If it's a top-level state—which 'home' is because it has no parent state–then its parent template is index.html. Right now, the 'home' state won't ever be activated. So let's see how we can activate a state.
